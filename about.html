Hybrid*

* I was recently called a "Hybrid" during a UX designers debate and that is the core of who i am as a person. I am both a designer and developer and therefore I understand how the two worlds influence each other which is very creative but also very effective. As a developer I think about the edge cases and as a designer I can design every detail early on.

I strive for the unity of design and technology. Being highly visual, I approach web development from a design first point of view and frontend development is where I thrive. I love to invest time in building modern UI that fits every viewport. I have a background in technology, robotics and electronics, music and art. As an tech nerd with a playful artistic mindset, I love to challenge traditional paths and develop new ways of visual communication. I play my piano daily: it's like a meditation for me. I also occasionally work on music and sound design for film, 3D interactive installations and theatre.

Trivia: I write with my left hand but in the opposite direction, mimicking a right hand handwriting.
